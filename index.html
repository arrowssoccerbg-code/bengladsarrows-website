About Us

Benglads Arrows Soccer Academy (BASA) develops disciplined, skilled, and educated footballers in Ghana.

Vision

To become a leading grassroots football academy in the Central Region and across Ghana.

Mission

To identify, train, and mentor young football talents through professional coaching, discipline, and exposure.
